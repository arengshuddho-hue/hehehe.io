About us

At Jaba Restaurant in Green Road Dhaka, Bangladesh, we pride ourselves on being the go-to destination for those who love authentic traditional food. Our mission is to share the rich flavors and culture of the Garo community in a warm and welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels at home.

    A traditional Garo-style chicken stew. Chicken is simmered with rice flour ('fura') to create a naturally thick, hearty, and jhal jhal (textured) gravy, all without a single drop of oil.

    The essence of chicken, purified. Tender chicken, steamed or boiled with nothing but a paste of ginger, onion, and chili. No oil, no complex spices. Taste the true, honest flavor of the bird.

    A beloved Chakma delicacy. A whole chicken, slow-cooked to fall-off-the-bone tenderness in a traditional clay pot with minimal spices.

  • Kabab Chatni
